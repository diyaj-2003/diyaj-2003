### hey, I'm Diya 👋

I'm a Biomedical Engineering graduate from King's College London, working at the intersection of **computational modelling**, **signal processing**, and **cardiac electrophysiology**.

My thesis investigated virtual pacemapping — using cardiac digital twins to localise the source of ventricular tachycardia non-invasively, by aligning simulated and clinical 12-lead ECGs.

Outside the lab I play **piano**, make **digital art**, build **websites**, and have a lot of opinions about music.
